Housing and Transportation

Please send a message to info@capoeiradetroit.org or use the contact form below if you need housing or transportation. Please note that housing is limited and we will do our very best to accommodate you. If you need a ride to and from the airport, bus station, train station etc please send a message with your travel itinerary.
